Privacy Policy

Baqqi Waqt — Prayer Time Countdown

Last updated: September 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Baqqi Waqt ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the Baqqi Waqt mobile application. By using the App you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Baqqi Waqt is designed to be minimal and privacy-friendly. We only collect the information necessary for the app to provide prayer times and related notifications.

Location Information

Why: To calculate local prayer times accurately.

How: We request access to your device location. The App uses either:

  • Device-level location (GPS/network) when you grant permission, or
  • Manual location entry (city / coordinates) if you prefer not to grant location permission.

Storage: Location data is stored only on your device and is not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly enable a feature that requires uploading (none enabled by default).

Notifications

Why: To send prayer reminder notifications.

How: The app asks for permission to show local notifications. Notification tokens or service identifiers are not collected unless you explicitly enable cloud notification features (not enabled by default).

Device & Usage Information (Optional)

By default, Baqqi Waqt does not collect analytics or personally identifiable information. If you or a future update enables analytics or crash reporting, that will be disclosed in an updated version of this policy.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and personalize prayer time calculations and countdowns.
  • Deliver local notification reminders for upcoming prayers (only with your permission).
  • Support and troubleshoot the app if you contact us (you may provide additional information in support requests).

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share limited information with third parties only in the following cases:

  • When required by law or to respond to lawful requests (e.g., court orders).
  • To protect the rights, property or safety of the App, our users, or the public.

If you enable optional features (for example, cloud backup, analytics, advertising), we will clearly disclose which third-party services are used and what data they collect in an updated policy and in-app settings.

4. Data Retention & Storage

Most data used by the App—location (if used), settings, and prayer schedules—are stored locally on your device. We do not retain personal data on external servers by default.

If you enable any cloud features (not enabled by default), the retention period will be shown in the feature description and you may request deletion as described below.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information stored locally and transmitted by the App. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. You acknowledge and accept the risk when using mobile apps.

6. Your Choices & Rights

Location: You can deny or revoke location permissions in your device settings. If you revoke location permission, you can still use the App by entering your location manually.

Notifications: You can choose to enable or disable notifications in-app or through your device settings.

Data Access & Deletion: If we collect any personal data on servers (only if you enable optional cloud features), you may contact us to request access, rectification, or deletion of your data. See the Contact section below.

7. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child provided us with personal information, contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

8. International Transfers

The App is provided globally. If any data is transferred outside your country (only applicable if you enable cloud features), we will follow applicable laws and take reasonable safeguards to protect your information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Major changes will be notified via the app or through the Play Store release notes.
